About this Sim

What is a Legendary?

Legendary weapons are a set of high-end weapons that have unique appearances. They have the same stats as ascended weapons, however they also have functionality to allow their inherent stat combo to be changed to any other stat combo when out of combat. They are crafted in the Mystic Forge. Each legendary weapon requires four components: a specific exotic precursor weapon, a Gift of Fortune, a Gift of Mastery and a final gift specific to the desired legendary weapon. For more info, refer to the Wiki.

Purpose of this Sim

This sim aims to emulate the in-game activity of generating Legendaries in the Mystic Forge. By running this application, the player can get insight on how Legendaries are generated and what happens when an attempt fails. Precursor data regarding sell/buy values is currently retrieved and updated from the official GW2 api.

Can you turn this process into Gold??

Short Answer: Yes! Long answer, refer to Law of Large Numbers. I have personally NOT used this process to make gold, but others have. As the original poster in the reddit mentioned, precursor crafting CAN be used to effectively make gold, but there is a very real chance of making a loss.

Why should i trust this Sim?

Generally speaking, you don't have to. If you are here, chances are that you are a person who likes to personally find out whether numbers add up or not. You are more than welcome to check out the code, report bugs and contribute in development.

What else?

I got the algorithm behind this sim from this reddit post. For the sake of simplicity, some numbers/percentages have been normalized and or rounded up. If you have data for this sim, don't hesitate to share!
