About this Sim

What is a Mystic Clover?

Mystic Clovers are used to create legendary weapons. They are generated in the Mystic Forge using one of two recipes and are rewarded in various PvP Reward Tracks. They are also dropped from the Chest of Legendary Crafting Materials. For more information, refer to the Wiki.

Purpose of this Sim

This sim aims to emulate the in-game activity of generating Mystic clovers using one of two recipes (1 or 10 Mystic Clovers at a time). By running this application, the player can get insight on how Mystic Clovers are generated and what happens when a Mystic Clover attempt fails. Data regarding material prices is retrieved and updated dynamically from the official GW2 api.

Can you turn this process into Gold??

Short Answer: Potentially. Long answer, refer to Law of Large Numbers. I have personally attempted this process to make gold when the process fails to generate Mystic Clovers and instead generates materials, which can be sold in the Tradepost. Unfortunately, it does not seem like a viable approach as the profits are usually not enough, especially after considering the Tradepost selling fees.

Why should i trust this Sim?

Generally speaking, you don't have to. If you are here, chances are that you are a person who likes to personally find out whether numbers add up or not. You are more than welcome to check out the code, report bugs and contribute in development.

What else?

I got the drop rates for all materials that can be generated for this sim from Wikipedia. For the sake of simplicity, some numbers/percentages have been normalized and or rounded up. If you have data for this sim, don't hesitate to share!
