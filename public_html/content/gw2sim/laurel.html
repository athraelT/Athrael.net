About this Sim

What is a Laurel?

Laurels are an account-bound non-tradeable currency that players can use to purchase items from laurel merchants, located in each major city. Laurels can be obtained as login rewards. The amount of laurels a player holds can be viewed by hovering over the '+' sign in the lower right of the inventory window, or from the Wallet tab in the Hero panel. For more info, refer to the Wiki.

Purpose of this Sim

This sim aims to emulate the in-game activity of generating Gold by buying Heavy Crafting Bags with Laurels, opening them for T6 Materials and selling them in the Tradepost. By running this application, players can get insight on approximately how much gold they can generate, given a number of Crafting bugs. Crafting Material data regarding sell/buy values is currently retrieved and updated from the official GW2 api.

Can you turn this process into Gold??

Absolutely; This is what this process all about. Amount of gold earned, however depends on the current state of the market.

Why should i trust this Sim?

Generally speaking, you don't have to. If you are here, chances are that you are a person who likes to personally find out whether numbers add up or not. You are more than welcome to check out the code, report bugs and contribute in development.

What else?

For the sake of simplicity, some numbers/percentages may have been normalized and or rounded up. If you have data for this sim, don't hesitate to share!
