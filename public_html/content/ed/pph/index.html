About the Powerplay Herald

The Powerplay Herald is an application, dedicated in informing players about each Leader's performance in the 5 major Powerplay activities of Elite Dangerous (Preparation, Fortification, Expansion, Undermining, Opposition) and providing a weekly Analysis, Guesses & Predictions in an informal, humorous manner.

Power Ranking

Power Ranking allows players to choose a Powerplay activity and view a chart with the performance of each Power leader in that activity. Data is available since Cycle 2 and is updated weekly.

Analysis & Predictions

Analysis & Predictions is a section where Cadoc, the original Author of the popular Powerplay Activity Analysis has shared his view of each Faction leader's weekly performance, as well as his guesses & assumptions for cycles 2-13. In the following weeks and due to Cadoc's departure from the project, a team is assembled to take on the task of continuing the weekly Powerplay Analysis.
